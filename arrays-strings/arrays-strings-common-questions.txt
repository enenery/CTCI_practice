Arrays and Strings Common Questions

-Given two strings, write a method/function to decide if one is a permutation(anagrams) of each other.
*Anagrams (Permutation): a word, phrase, or name formed by rearranging the letters of another.
ex) cinema <-> iceman, abc <-> cab

Palindrom: a word phrase, or sequence that reads the same backwards as forward.
ex) madam, anna
